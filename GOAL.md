# Arkey AI

Arkey AI is a cutting-edge meeting assistant designed to enhance productivity and collaboration during virtual meetings. Leveraging advanced speech recognition and natural language processing technologies, Arkey AI can transcribe conversations, summarize key points, and provide real-time insights to participants (provide answers, key questions to ask, and strategically drive the dicussion toward goals).

## Key Features

- **Real-time Transcription**: Arkey AI captures and transcribes spoken content during meetings, ensuring that no important information is missed.
- **Real-time Insights**: Arkey AI analyzes the conversation in real-time, offering users
        - answers to the questions asked by other participants,
        - key questions to ask
        - strategies to drive the discussion toward goals.
- **Intelligent Summarization**: After each meeting, Arkey AI generates concise summaries, highlighting key decisions and action items.
- **Contextual Assistance**: By understanding the context of discussions, Arkey AI can provide relevant information and suggestions to participants.
- **Seamless Integration**: Arkey AI easily integrates with popular video conferencing platforms, making it a valuable addition to any virtual meeting environment.
- **Manual Intervention**: Users can provide inputs using voice commands or text inputs to guide Arkey AI's responses and actions.
- **Knowledge aware answers**: Arkey AI responses are based on the materials supplied by the user, ensuring that the information is relevant and accurate. 

## Functional Requirements
- Auto detect speaker audio content
- Users should see message displaying the "Listening..." status.
- Provide a way to upload meeting materials (e.g., documents, slides) for context.
- Provide a way to choose materials for context (e.g., previous meeting notes, relevant documents).
- Provide a way to pass text and speech inputs.


## Technical Requirements
- Use Electron
- Use Anthropic API
- Windows support
- Windows installer
